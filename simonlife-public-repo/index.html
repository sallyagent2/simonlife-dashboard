Capsule Law

What the day must preserve

Explicit over implicit

No silent carryover, no assumed menu, no missing block that is "understood." If it happened, it gets recorded.

Sunday decides the week

The week is only real if Sunday tests it. Passing Sunday is what earns the next rung.

Ground truth over narrative

The capsule is the historical truth layer. The dashboard should make it easier to feed that truth, not replace it.
